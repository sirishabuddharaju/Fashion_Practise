What is Fashion Design Meaning?

Fashion Design is a techno-commercial art that involves creating clothing and accessories that exemplify aesthetics, functionality, and social and cultural sense. Fashion Designers are responsible for conceptualising, designing and creating clothes, accessories and footwear.They consider a range of factors like colour palettes, texture, silhouette and many others to design incredible products.

What is Fashion Design

Fashion Design is an art and science that engages creativity, logical thinking, and responsibility while designing clothes, accessories and footwear.
